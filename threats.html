Common Cyber Threats

🎣 Phishing

Fake emails or websites that trick you into revealing sensitive info.

🐛 Malware

Malicious software designed to damage or steal data from your system.

💰 Ransomware

Locks your files and demands payment to unlock them.

🔓 Data Breach

Unauthorized access to personal or organizational data.

👥 Social Engineering

Tricking users into giving up confidential information using deception.

🛑 Denial of Service

Flooding a server to crash it or make it unavailable to users.
