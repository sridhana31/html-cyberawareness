Top Cyber Safety Tips

🔐 Use Strong Passwords

Always use complex, unique passwords for each site. Avoid personal info and use a mix of characters.

📲 Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Add an extra layer of protection beyond your password — especially for email and banking.

⚠️ Don’t Click Suspicious Links

Hover over links before clicking. Never trust unexpected emails with attachments or urgent messages.

🔄 Keep Software Updated

Updates fix security bugs. Keep your OS, browser, and antivirus software up to date.

🛡️ Use Antivirus & Firewalls

These tools detect and block malicious software before it harms your device.

📶 Be Cautious on Public Wi-Fi

Never access sensitive accounts on open Wi-Fi. Use a VPN for added security.
