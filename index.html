What is Cyber Awareness?

Cyber Awareness is the understanding of digital threats and how to stay protected online. It's about being aware of the risks and taking steps to prevent cyber attacks, identity theft, and data loss.

Why is it Important?

  • 🌐 Your personal and financial data is always online.
  • 🕵️ Cyber attackers target people of all ages and roles.
  • 💸 Threats can cause data leaks, financial losses, and reputation damage.
  • 📉 Awareness helps prevent digital disasters before they happen.

Common Cyber Threats

  • Phishing: Fake emails that steal your login info.
  • Malware: Software that harms your system.
  • Ransomware: Locks your data and demands payment.
  • Data Breaches: Hackers stealing personal or business data.
  • Social Engineering: Tricking people into giving away data.

How to Stay Safe Online

  1. Use strong and unique passwords.
  2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).
  3. Don’t click suspicious links or emails.
  4. Keep your software updated regularly.
  5. Be cautious when using public Wi-Fi.
